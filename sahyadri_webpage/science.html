Key Highlights

Selected results demonstrating the suite's capabilities.

1
Cosmic Web Evolution
Visualizations tracking structure formation from z=5.6 to z=0, revealing the emergence of filaments, walls, and voids.
2
Omega_m Dependence
Clean measurement of how matter density affects the matter power spectrum and halo mass function across non-linear scales.
3
VVF Cosmological Sensitivity
First demonstration that Voronoi Volume Functions are sensitive probes of cosmological parameters in the non-linear regime.
4
Environment–Property Correlations
Quantified correlations between halo environment metrics and internal properties like concentration and spin.
5
Validation
Excellent agreement with analytic predictions from Halofit (power spectrum) and Tinker (mass function) fitting formulae.
