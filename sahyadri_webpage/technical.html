Redshift Coverage

101 snapshots provide dense temporal coverage from early times to the present day.

z = 12
z = 0

The primary science target lies at z < 0.5, corresponding to the volume probed by DESI BGS and 4MOST surveys. Dense snapshot spacing in this regime enables detailed studies of late-time cosmic evolution, merger histories, and environmental processes.

Computing and analysis

All simulations were performed on the Pegasus cluster at IUCAA, Pune. A detailed description of the end-to-end pipeline for performing the simulations and generating post-processing products can be found at the Sahyadri code repository. We gratefully acknowledge HPC facilities at IUCAA, Pune and TIFR, Mumbai.
